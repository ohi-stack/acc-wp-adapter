# Algonquian Real Estate Platform Source Document

## Purpose

This document defines the source architecture for AlgonquianRealEstate.com as a WordPress-based real estate acquisition, transaction, marketplace, education, and digital product platform.

The system is designed to acquire deals, underwrite opportunities, generate offers, distribute deals to buyers, manage signatures, archive executed documents, track closing, sell digital products, and support recurring revenue.

## Operating Position

Algonquian Real Estate LLC should use disciplined commercial language. The platform supports real estate acquisition and software operations. It should not represent guaranteed returns, securities offerings, governmental authority, or compulsory systems.

## Core System Functions

- Capture seller leads
- Create structured deal records
- Underwrite deals through MAO calculations
- Generate offers and contracts
- Move deals through a pipeline board
- Match deals to buyers
- Accept bids and buyer commitments
- Generate assignment agreements
- Send signature requests
- Archive signed documents
- Track escrow and closing
- Sell digital products
- Sell buyer memberships
- Support education and LMS revenue
- Track analytics and revenue performance

## Revenue Strategy

AlgonquianRealEstate.com should not only present the company. It should function as a money-making operating system.

Primary monetization channels:

1. Buyer marketplace memberships
2. Premium deal access
3. Digital products and templates
4. MAO and offer-generator SaaS subscriptions
5. Funding marketplace or lender matching
6. Education platform memberships
7. Contract template store
8. Buyer bidding fees or deposits
9. Transaction coordination or processing fees
10. White-label licensing of the plugin suite

## Production Standard

Every module must be:

- Operational
- Documented
- Repeatable
- Testable
- Permission-controlled
- Logged
- Backed up
- Maintainable
