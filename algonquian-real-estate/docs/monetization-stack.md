# Monetization Stack

AlgonquianRealEstate.com should be built as a revenue-producing platform, not only as a brochure site.

## Primary Revenue Channels

1. Buyer marketplace memberships
2. Premium deal access
3. Buyer bidding and bid deposits
4. Digital product sales
5. Contract template store
6. MAO and offer-generator SaaS subscriptions
7. Education/LMS memberships
8. Funding marketplace and lender matching
9. Transaction coordination or processing fees
10. White-label plugin licensing

## Digital Products

Initial digital products should include:

- MAO calculator package
- Wholesale contract pack
- Seller financing template package
- Subject-to offer worksheet
- Due diligence checklist pack
- Acquisition SOP package
- Lender package templates
- Connecticut landlord document pack

## Subscription Products

- Basic buyer access
- Pro buyer access
- Institutional buyer access
- Investor education membership
- AI offer generator subscription

## Payment Layer

Use Stripe Checkout first for digital products, buyer memberships, reservation fees, bid deposits, and document-processing fees. Add Stripe Connect only if the platform later routes funds to third-party sellers, partners, or service providers.
